Privacy Policy

At Haseeb Naqash's Portfolio, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

We take your trust seriously and strive to maintain the highest standards of data protection for all our visitors and clients.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a better experience and respond to your inquiries effectively.

Personal Information

When you reach out via our contact forms or services, we may collect:

  • Full Name

  • Project Requirements

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your experience and deliver high-quality services, including:

Responding to your inquiries and project requests
Improving website functionality and user experience
Providing technical support and updates
Optimizing search engine rankings and site speed

Communicating important project milestones
04

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We value your privacy above all else. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties.

With Your Consent

We may share your data with strategic partners or service providers only when you provide explicit consent to do so for a specific project or service.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Preventing fraud or unauthorized access
  • Complying with legal proceedings or court orders
  • Protecting the intellectual property of our website
  • Ensuring public safety and platform security
05

Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to ensure your data remains protected from unauthorized access:

Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols during transfer and storage.

Access Control

Only authorized personnel can access account data to ensure maximum privacy.

Data Protection

We regularly monitor our systems for potential vulnerabilities and attacks.

You have full control over your personal information and can exercise the following rights:

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons.
